ABOUT US

Hi! SoDelicious began in 2021, we aim to give you a recipe experience unlike anything you have seen before. We want to inspire you to make African, Asian, Caribbean and Indigenous inspired recipes. We also want to encourage you to try the restaurants recommended on this website.



An important detail about our website is that we have all ingredients within the directions to make it easier to prepare the recipe while using your device.
